## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Ad Performance (CPC > CHF 2)
**Response:**
- Pause underperforming ads immediately
- Test new creative (images, copy)
- Adjust targeting (narrow interests)
- Shift budget to organic/partnership channels

### Risk 2: Partnership Rejection
**Response:**
- Offer better terms (higher referral fee)
- Provide more value (free tools for installers)
- Target different partners (housing cooperatives)
- Direct outreach as backup

### Risk 3: Media Ignores Press Releases
**Response:**
- Personal outreach to journalists
- Offer exclusive interview
- Create newsworthy event (formation milestone)
- Paid advertorial (last resort)

### Risk 4: Referral Program Underperforms
**Response:**
- Increase incentive (CHF 75 vs. CHF 50)
- Simplify sharing (one-click WhatsApp)
- Leaderboard with prizes
- Time-limited bonus

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Personality Traits
- **Friendly:** Neighbor helping neighbor
- **Clear:** No jargon, simple German
- **Trustworthy:** Transparent, honest about process
- **Empowering:** "You can do this"
- **Local:** Baden pride, community focus

### Writing Guidelines
- Use "Sie" (formal you) in official docs
- Use "du" (informal you) in social media
- Short sentences (< 20 words)
- Active voice, not passive
- Numbers over words (15 Rp vs. f√ºnfzehn Rappen)
- Emojis sparingly (1-2 per post max)

### Avoid
- Energy industry jargon
- Overpromising savings
- Attacking Regionalwerke
- Political statements
- Hype language ("revolutionary", "disruptive")
