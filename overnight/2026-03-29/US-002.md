# US-002 — Expose municipality demand in /admin/strategy

**Date:** 2026-03-29  
**Story:** US-002  
**Status:** ✅ Implemented

---

## Summary

Added a new `/admin/strategy` operator surface that fetches verified municipality demand signals from `insights_engine.compute_municipality_demand_signal()` and presents them as an actionable ranked table. Municipalities are sorted by demand level (high → medium → low → none) so the highest-priority municipalities appear first. High and medium demand municipalities are visually flagged with an "▶ Act now" prompt and a coloured left-border stripe, making it immediately obvious which municipalities should be acted on next. The route returns HTML when the `Accept: text/html` header is present and falls back to JSON, keeping the surface compatible with programmatic consumers.
